Foie Gras



Time to desicrate this poor goose! This french favourite will
have your dinner guests holding their heads in shame and is guaranteed
to make everyone feel just terrible about themselves.


Ingredients

  • Goose
  • It's Liver
  • Funnel
  • Butter

Method

  1. Tie down the goose using your finest cable ties. Make sure to use a
    grubby table to cause infections!
  2. Force the funnel down it's poor little throat as far as you can.
  3. Melt your butter in a large pan, ensuring not to burn it.
  4. Pour the butter gently down the gooses throat, ensuring to muffle
    any honking and making sure it doesn't move.
  5. Finally, crush it's head with a mallet. Dissect the goose and
    remove it's liver.
  6. Finally, add it's liver to the blender.
  7. Eat.
  8. Finally, enjoy!
