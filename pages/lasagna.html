Lasagna



A disgusting lasagna, made with the finest pubic hairs, and milk made from
the most overworked horse in the world's jizz. This recipe follows a process of
neglect and half-arsedery that is guaranteed to make your eyes water.


Ingredients

  • La
  • Sag
  • Na
  • Horse Jizz
  • Dog Pubes
  • Unsalted Biddy Pubes
  • Brown Sauce

Method

  1. Bring a pot of water to a low boil, and delicately add the
    dog pubes and biddy pubes (I prefer unsalted!) one by one to
    the pot.
  2. Add your horse jizz (the fresher the better!) to the broth
    and add brown sauce, stirring in to tastes.
  3. After you remove your cheapest lasagna from the microwave,
    put it in the sink and proceed to pour the now boiled mixture
    over the lasagna, ensuring you ruin every square inch.
  4. Leave to cool for 8 eons, and enjoy!
